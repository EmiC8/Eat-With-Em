Bacon Egg Salad

A simple healthy meal of veggie base topped with delicious proteins such as corn, avocado egg, and bacon. The additional mayonnaise adds even more flavoring. A salad serves as a perfect dish before the gym. Let's try out this unique salad!

prep time

5 minutes

cook time

10 minutes

serving

1 serving

Tags: breakfast lunch dinner drinks

instructions

Step 1

Wash all the veggies and then chop it into small pieces

Step 2

Cooked the bacon for 7 minutes

Step 3

Placed all the ingredients into the bowl. Then add cheese, croutons, mayonnaise on top.

ingredients

bacon, corn, egg, croutons, mayonnaise, avocado
